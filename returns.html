Return & Refund Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026. This policy explains how returns, exchanges, and refunds are handled for TridentWear clothing orders within India.

Privacy Policy Terms & Conditions Return & Refund Policy Shipping Policy Contact Us

1. Return Window

Eligible clothing products may be returned within 7 days from the date of delivery.

2. Return Conditions

  • The product must be unused, unwashed, and not worn beyond trial fitting.
  • All original tags, labels, and packaging must remain intact.
  • The item must be returned in saleable condition.

3. Accepted Return Reasons

  • Wrong size received or eligible size exchange request
  • Damaged product delivered
  • Wrong item delivered

4. Refund Timeline

Once the returned product is received and approved after quality check, eligible refunds are processed within 5 to 7 working days to the original payment method. Bank or payment gateway timelines may vary.

5. Exchange Option

Exchange requests are available for eligible clothing items, subject to stock availability and product inspection. If the requested replacement is unavailable, a refund or alternate resolution may be offered.

6. Non-Returnable Items

Custom printed products are non-returnable unless the item delivered is damaged, defective, or different from what was ordered.

7. How to Start a Return

Please contact us with your order number, product details, and the reason for return. If the item is damaged or incorrect, please share clear photos to help us review the request faster.

Quick Summary

  • 7-day return window for eligible clothing items.
  • Items must be unused with tags intact.
  • Exchange option is available for eligible products.
  • Approved refunds are processed in 5 to 7 working days.
