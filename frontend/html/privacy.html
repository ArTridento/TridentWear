Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026. This policy explains how TridentWear collects, uses, shares, stores, and protects customer information for orders placed in India.

Privacy Policy Terms & Conditions Return & Refund Policy Shipping Policy Contact Us

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order, create an account, contact support, or request a return, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, billing address, order details, size selections, and support messages.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process, confirm, and deliver orders
  • To handle exchanges, returns, refunds, and customer support
  • To send order updates, shipping details, and service communication
  • To maintain records, prevent fraud, and comply with applicable Indian law

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited information with payment gateway providers, courier and delivery partners, website technology providers, and authorities where disclosure is legally required.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational safeguards such as controlled admin access, secure password handling, and protected customer records to reduce the risk of unauthorised access, misuse, or alteration.

5. Your Rights

Subject to applicable Indian law, you may request access to your data, correction of inaccurate data, or deletion of data that is no longer required, unless we must retain it for legal, tax, compliance, or dispute purposes.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be published on this page with the updated effective date.

Quick Summary

  • We collect only the information needed to run the store and deliver orders.
  • We share limited data only with payment, logistics, and service partners.
  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of eligible data.
