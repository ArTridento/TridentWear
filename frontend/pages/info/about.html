Our Story

We made the clothes we wanted to wear.

TridentWear started with a simple frustration: most clothes looked fine but felt forgettable. The fabric was light, the fit was off, or the whole thing felt made to be replaced too quickly.

We wanted pieces with more weight and more intent — clean silhouettes, solid materials, and a look that feels personal the moment you put it on. That's the standard behind every piece we put out.

What We Stand For

The Trident Standard

Fabric

Oversized, classic, and slim fits — each graded for Indian shoulder widths and torso lengths. No more guessing. Just pick your size and it fits.

Designed and manufactured in India. We work with local production units to keep quality high, turnaround fast, and support domestic craftsmanship.

Mission

Streetwear that earns its place in your wardrobe.

We're not chasing trends. TridentWear is about building a small, focused catalog of essentials — T-shirts that anchor any outfit, absorb everyday life, and still look clean after a year of use.

No seasonal drops for the sake of it. No fast-fashion weight. Just solid pieces, restocked when they run out.
