Shipping Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026. This policy explains our delivery timelines, shipping charges, COD support, tracking, and delay handling for orders within India.

Privacy Policy Terms & Conditions Return & Refund Policy Shipping Policy Contact Us

1. Delivery Time

Orders are generally delivered within 3 to 7 business days from dispatch, depending on location, stock readiness, courier coverage, and order volume.

2. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges may be free, fixed, or calculated at checkout depending on the order value, promotions, selected shipping method, and destination pin code.

3. Cash on Delivery

Cash on Delivery (COD) may be available for eligible orders and serviceable Indian pin codes. COD availability may be restricted based on location, order value, or previous failed deliveries.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, tracking details may be shared by email, SMS, WhatsApp, or your account area where available. Tracking updates depend on courier partner systems.

5. Delay Conditions

Delivery may be delayed due to weather, strikes, festivals, public holidays, remote service areas, courier issues, government restrictions, force majeure events, or incorrect address information provided by the customer.

6. Failed Delivery Attempts

If delivery cannot be completed due to incorrect address details, repeated non-availability, or refusal to accept delivery, the order may be returned to origin. Additional re-shipping charges may apply where legally permitted.

Quick Summary

  • Standard delivery time is 3 to 7 business days.
  • Shipping may be free, fixed, or calculated at checkout.
  • COD is available only on eligible orders and locations.
  • Tracking is shared after dispatch.
